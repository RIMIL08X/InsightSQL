1️⃣ Revenue & Sales Questions (MOST IMPORTANT)
    • Which product generated the most revenue?
    • What is the total revenue per product?
    • What is the total revenue across all orders?
    • Which products have generated more than 500 in revenue?
    • Rank products by total revenue in descending order
    • What is the average revenue per order?
    • Which product has the lowest total revenue?
    • What percentage of total revenue comes from the top 3 products?

2️⃣ Orders & Transaction Analysis
    • How many orders are there in total?
    • How many orders were placed per product?
    • Which product has the highest number of orders?
    • What is the average order value?
    • List orders along with their payment amounts
    • Which orders have payments greater than 200?
    • How many orders have been paid successfully?

3️⃣ Inventory Questions (VERY STABLE)
    • Which products have inventory less than 10?
    • Which products are out of stock?
    • List all products with their current inventory levels
    • Which product has the highest inventory?
    • Which products need restocking?
    • What is the total inventory count across all products?

4️⃣ Customer-Focused Questions
    • How many customers are there?
    • List all customers
    • Which customer placed the most orders?
    • How many orders did each customer place?
    • Which customers have placed more than one order?
    • List customers along with their total spending

5️⃣ Product Catalog Questions
    • List all products
    • What are the available products?
    • Show product names with their prices
    • Which products cost more than 500?
    • What is the most expensive product?
    • What is the cheapest product?

6️⃣ Payment Analysis (GOOD FOR DEMO)
    • What is the total payment amount?
    • List all payments
    • Which order has the highest payment?
    • What is the average payment amount?
    • Which payments are linked to which products?
    • How much revenue was generated from each order?

7️⃣ Combined / Slightly Advanced (Still Safe)
    • Show products with their total revenue and inventory
    • Which products have high revenue but low inventory?
    • Compare inventory levels with sales revenue per product
    • Which products sell well but have low stock?
    • Show product name, total orders, and total revenue

8️⃣ Questions to AVOID (Will Break Things)
Do NOT ask these unless backend is upgraded:
    • “Why did revenue drop last month?”
    • “Predict next month’s sales”
    • “Which product is trending?”
    • “Explain customer behavior”
    • “Show revenue growth over time”
These require time-series, ML, or text analysis you don’t have.

9️⃣ Best Demo Questions (Use These First)
If this is for review / internship:
    1. Which product generated the most revenue?
    2. What is the total revenue per product?
    3. Which products have inventory less than 10?
    4. Which customer placed the most orders?
    5. Show products with their total revenue and inventory
